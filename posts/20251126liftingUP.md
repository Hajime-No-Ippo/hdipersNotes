# The Limit of "Lifting Up"

## 1. The Clever Little Girl
I suddenly thought of our 18-month-old daughter today. She is smart, adorable, and showing so much potential.
A huge part of my grind here in Ireland is to "lift her up"—to give her a better starting point.
But based on my experiences over the last two months, I've realized something sobering: **The "lift" we can provide is limited.**
Money, a house, a passport—these are just platforms. They are not happiness itself.

[END]

[ZH]
# 托举的极限

## 1. 那个聪明的小女孩
今天突然想到了我们一岁半的宝宝。她现在表现得很聪明，很可爱，充满了潜力。
我在爱尔兰这么拼命，很大一部分原因是为了“托举”她——给她一个更好的起点。
但结合我最近两个多月的经历和认知，我清醒地意识到一点：**我们能做的“托举”，真的是有限的。**
钱、房子、护照——这些只是平台，它们本身不是幸福。
[END]

[EN]
## 2. The Formula for Happiness
My recent journey has taught me a formula:
**Happiness comes from the realization of a significant goal through hard work.**
It’s not the result that brings lasting joy; it’s the process of conquering something difficult.
If I just hand her a comfortable life, I might actually be robbing her of the chance to find this happiness.
The question isn't "How much can I give her?", but "How can she learn to achieve this for herself?"

[END]

[ZH]
# 2. 幸福的公式
我最近的旅程教给了我一个公式：
**幸福来自于经过努力的大目标的实现。**
带来的持久快乐的不是结果本身，而是征服困难的过程。
如果我只是直接塞给她一个舒适的生活，我可能反而在剥夺她寻找这种幸福的机会。
所以问题不在于“我能给她多少”，而在于“她如何学会自己去实现它？”
[END]

[EN]
## 3. Teaching the "Method", Not the "Result"
So, my philosophy has shifted.
While we are pursuing our own happiness (my wife and I working hard here), we are modeling the path for her.
Our ultimate goal is to guide her to master two things:
1.  **The ability to "happily go all in":** To find something she loves enough to work tirelessly for, entering that state of flow.
2.  **The courage to accept failure:** To understand that failure is not the end, but a signal to push harder and go further.

If she has these tools, she won't need me to lift her. She will fly on her own.

[END]

[ZH]
# 3. 教“方法”，而不是给“结果”
所以，我的想法变了。
当我们在追求自己的幸福时（我和老婆在这里努力奋斗），我们其实是在给她做演示。
我们的终极目标是引导她掌握两样东西：
1.  **“愉快地全力以赴”的能力：** 找到一件她足够热爱的事，愿意不知疲倦地投入，进入那种心流状态。
2.  **接受失败的勇气：** 让她明白失败不是终点，而是让她更加奋勇向前的信号。

如果她掌握了这些工具，她就不需要我的托举了。她自己就会飞。
[END]
