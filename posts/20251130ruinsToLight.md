# The War Room

People ask me what it's like starting over at 43 in a new country. They imagine a sleek university campus and bright cafes.

This is my reality.

![The War Room|600](https://assets.flowstates.me/2025/war_room_laptop.jpg)

This is not just a cheap rental room; this is my **War Room**.
Exposed beams, crumbling plaster, dust, and construction tools—this is the raw, unfiltered environment where I fight my daily battles with algorithms and system design.

The contrast is sharp: the chaos of the physical world versus the precise, clean logic of the Java code on my screen. The cold draft from the window keeps me awake. The dust reminds me that comfort is the enemy of progress.

I don't need a fancy desk. I need a compiler that works and a brain that refuses to quit.

[END]

[ZH]
# 战地指挥部

人们常问我，43 岁在异国他乡重头开始是什么感觉。他们想象的是光鲜亮丽的大学校园和明亮的咖啡馆。

而这，才是我的现实。

![战地指挥部|600](https://assets.flowstates.me/2025/war_room_laptop.jpg)

这不仅仅是一间廉价的群租房；这是我的**战地指挥部**。
裸露的房梁、摇摇欲坠的石膏墙、灰尘和满地的施工工具——这就是我每天与算法和系统设计进行肉搏战的原始环境。

这种对比是极其尖锐的：物理世界的混乱不堪，对抗着我屏幕上 Java 代码那精确、整洁的逻辑。窗户缝里透进来的冷风让我保持清醒。满屋的灰尘提醒我，舒适是进步的敌人。

我不需要花哨的书桌。我只需要一个能跑通的编译器，和一个拒绝放弃的大脑。
[END]

[EN]
# The Stairway to Light

Every day, I navigate the mess of my current situation. It feels like climbing a steep, dark staircase covered in rubble.

![The Stairway|600](https://assets.flowstates.me/2025/stairway_to_light.jpg)

This image is the perfect metaphor for my journey. The bottom is dark, cluttered with doubts, language barriers, and the sheer volume of new knowledge I must absorb. It's easy to trip. It's messy.

But look up.

At the top of those stairs is the light. It's the Eolas lab, it's the mastery of technology, it's the ability to build the AI of the future.

I am currently standing on the dark steps, covered in dust. But my eyes are fixed firmly on that doorway of light. Every line of code written, every bug crushed, is one step up this ladder.

The climb is hard. Good. That means I'm going the right way.

[END]

[ZH]
# 通往光明的阶梯

每一天，我都在应对眼下糟糕处境的一地鸡毛。这感觉就像在攀爬一段陡峭、黑暗、布满碎石的楼梯。

![通往光明的阶梯|600](https://assets.flowstates.me/2025/stairway_to_light.jpg)

这张图是我旅程的完美隐喻。底部是黑暗的，堆满了自我怀疑、语言障碍和我必须吸收的海量新知识。很容易绊倒，一片狼藉。

但是，往上看。

在楼梯的顶端是光。那是 Eolas 实验室，是对技术的掌控，是构建未来 AI 的能力。

我现在正站在布满灰尘的黑暗台阶上。但我的目光紧紧锁定在那扇光明的门上。每一行写下的代码，每一个消灭的 Bug，都是这架梯子上向上的一步。

攀登很艰难。很好。这意味着我走对了路。
[END]
