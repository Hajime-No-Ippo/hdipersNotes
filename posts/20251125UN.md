# The Dublin "United Nations" and the Missing Milk

## 1. The Battle Royale of the Kitchen
I moved back to my €400/month "Youth Hostel"—a polite name for a famous Dublin group rental.
The demographic here is specific: delivery riders and construction workers. The vibe is raw survival.
The kitchen is a battlefield. You put milk in the fridge? It’s gone. You buy meat or vegetables? They vanish. Even clothes in the dryer enter a different dimension.
It’s not malice; it’s just the law of the jungle.

[END]

# 都柏林“联合国”与消失的牛奶

## 1. 厨房里的大逃杀
我回到了我那月租400欧的“青年旅社”——这是对这间都柏林著名群租房的委婉称呼。
这里的住户画像很清晰：送外卖的小哥和建筑工地的兄弟。空气里弥漫着一种原始的生存气息。
厨房就是战场。你放进冰箱的牛奶？喝光了。你买的肉和菜？不翼而飞。甚至洗烘机里的衣服也会穿越到异次元。
这倒不是什么恶意，这只是此地的丛林法则。
[END]

[EN]
## 2. The 2:30 AM "English Lesson"
Last Thursday (or was it Friday?), I was woken up at 2:30 AM.
In my half-sleep, I heard incredibly fluent, authentic English swearing. It was so vivid I thought, *"Wow, my English dreams are getting realistic."*
I woke up to realize it wasn't a dream. The Malaysian guy and the Egyptian guy next door were in a full-blown fistfight.
The result? The Egyptian guy, who lost the fight, moved into my dorm room today.
So now, my room is an International League:
* Top bunk: A Frenchman.
* New roommate: The bruised Egyptian.
* Me: The Chinese guy coding in the corner.

[END]

[ZH]
# 2. 凌晨两点半的“英语课”
上周四（还是周五？），凌晨两点半。
我在睡梦中听到了一阵极其地道、流利的英语国骂（F-words满天飞）。那声音太真实了，我迷迷糊糊地想：*“嚯，我现在的英语梦做得挺逼真啊。”*
醒了才发现，那不是梦。是隔壁的马来人和埃及人打起来了。
战局的结果是：那个被打的埃及人，今天搬进了我的宿舍。
于是，我的房间现在正式组成了“国际联盟”：
* 上铺：一个法国人。
* 新室友：那个刚挨了揍的埃及人。
* 我：一个躲在角落里写代码的中国人。
[END]

[EN]
## 3. The Eye of the Storm (Epilogue)
It sounds chaotic. It *is* chaotic. But strangely, I find peace here.
While they fight over territory or stolen milk, I am in my own world. The noise becomes white noise. The chaos highlights my order.

This afternoon, I felt ashamed of almost exposing my weakness in class.
But tonight, sitting in this chaotic room, I unleashed a storm on my keyboard. Monotonic Queue, DFS, BFS—I typed them out as if possessed. It was terrifyingly efficient.
I proved one thing to myself: **There is nothing I cannot conquer past midnight.**
Let the world be noisy. My code is silent, logical, and unstoppable.

[END]

[ZH]
# 3. 风暴眼（尾声）
听起来很乱。确实很乱。但奇怪的是，我在这里找到了一种平静。
当他们为地盘或被偷的牛奶争吵时，我在我自己的世界里。噪音变成了白噪音，混乱反而衬托出了我内心的秩序。

今天下午，我还因为在课上差点露怯而感到羞愧。
但今晚，坐在这个混乱的房间里，我在键盘上掀起了一场风暴。单调队列、DFS、BFS——我像着了魔一样把它们敲了出来。那种效率简直令人恐惧。
我向自己证明了一件事：**没有我后半夜搞不定的事儿。**
让世界去吵吧。我的代码是安静的，逻辑严密的，且不可阻挡的。
